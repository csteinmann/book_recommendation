User perception on book recommendation systems

Hey, my name is Clemens, and I am currently studying Computer Science at the University of Koblenz. The following case study is being used for my bachelor's thesis, in which I am investigating the influences of user perceptions on recommendation systems. Specifically, my work is focused on book recommendation systems. Please familiarize yourself with the scenario provided below and then follow the further instructions. I thank you very much for your participation.

Research scenario

Imagine you are a big fan of fantasy books and are looking for a new book to read. To assist you, you are using the help of four different recommendation systems, each of which has created a top-10 list for you. These lists show books that are recommended based on your interests. The first book in each list represents the system's best-fit recommendation. Please carefully review the lists and then fill out the questionnaire. Thank you very much.
